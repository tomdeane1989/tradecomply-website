**Acclaim SSIP was renamed Once For All Health & Safety SSIP on 28 April 2025 —
the name and branding changed, but the health & safety assessment itself did
not.** If you hold an Acclaim certificate, it stays valid; if you're applying now,
you'll get the same assessment under the new name. It's still SSIP-approved and
still offers Deem to Satisfy.

## What actually changed

Purely the branding. The health & safety assessment content and requirements are
identical — Once For All Health & Safety is the same SSIP (Safety Schemes in
Procurement) certificate Acclaim was, with a new name, logo and colours. It
operates as a Constructionline product under the Once For All parent group.

<PlainEnglish>
Don't panic if you see "Once For All Health & Safety" where you expected
"Acclaim". It's the same thing. Main contractors should accept certificates under
either name.
</PlainEnglish>

## Is my Acclaim certificate still valid?

Yes. Acclaim certificates issued up to the rename date remain valid through their
normal expiry. Your status isn't affected — you'll simply receive the re-branded
version at renewal. The underlying assessment, and your
[Deem to Satisfy](/ssip/ssip-mutual-recognition-explained) recognition across
other SSIP schemes, are unchanged.

## How you get it

Once For All Health & Safety SSIP comes bundled with paid **Constructionline**
Gold and Platinum membership at no extra cost, and is also available standalone to
businesses that don't want full Constructionline membership. Pricing is based on
your annual turnover; there's no single published headline figure. If you're
weighing the Constructionline tiers, see
[Constructionline Gold vs Silver vs Bronze](/compare/constructionline-gold-vs-silver-vs-bronze).

<WatchOut title="It only covers health & safety">
Like any SSIP certificate, Once For All Health & Safety covers the core health &
safety assessment only — not financial vetting or the
[Common Assessment Standard](/ssip/common-assessment-standard-question-set). For
the wider picture of where SSIP stops, see [SSIP accreditation explained](/ssip).
</WatchOut>

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Acclaim still a thing?

Acclaim SSIP was rebranded to Once For All Health & Safety SSIP in April 2025. The
scheme continues — only the name and branding changed. Existing Acclaim
certificates remain valid.

### Is Once For All Health & Safety SSIP approved?

Yes. It's a member of SSIP, supported by HSE guidance, and offers Deem to Satisfy,
so it's recognised across other SSIP schemes.

### Do I need Constructionline to get it?

No. It's included free with paid Constructionline Gold and Platinum membership,
but it's also available standalone to businesses that don't hold Constructionline
membership.

### Did the assessment get harder after the rename?

No. The health & safety assessment content and requirements stayed identical — the
rename was a branding change only.
