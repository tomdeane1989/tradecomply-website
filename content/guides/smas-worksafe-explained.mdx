**SMAS (Safety Management Advisory Services) Worksafe is an SSIP-approved health
& safety scheme, with SSIP certificates starting from around £345.** It does the
same core job as CHAS or SafeContractor — proving your health & safety to a main
contractor — and it's often positioned as a lower-cost option for smaller firms.

## What SMAS Worksafe is

SMAS Worksafe is a member of SSIP (Safety Schemes in Procurement) and is
accredited by UKAS to ISO 17020, the highest level of assurance. Certificates are
**valid for 12 months**, and because it's SSIP-approved it offers
[Deem to Satisfy](/ssip/ssip-mutual-recognition-explained) — so a valid SMAS
certificate is recognised by other SSIP schemes, and vice versa.

<PlainEnglish>
SMAS clears the same SSIP health & safety bar as the bigger names. If a client
accepts "any SSIP scheme", SMAS counts — and it may be the cheapest way in for a
sole trader or micro business.
</PlainEnglish>

## SMAS and the Common Assessment Standard

SMAS offers the [Common Assessment Standard](/ssip/common-assessment-standard-question-set)
through its **Worksafe Pro** package — the broader pre-qualification standard that
covers far more than health & safety. As a recognised assessment body, SMAS
provides the Common Assessment Standard at the desktop level (the document-based
assessment, rather than a site visit). It's mandated for much public-sector work
under procurement policy note PPN 03/24.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is SMAS SSIP approved?

Yes. SMAS Worksafe is an SSIP member scheme, accredited by UKAS to ISO 17020, and
it offers Deem to Satisfy recognition with other SSIP schemes.

### How much does SMAS Worksafe cost?

SMAS publishes SSIP certificates "from around £345", with sole-trader and
higher-tier pricing quoted via its online quote tool. The page doesn't state VAT,
so confirm your own quote. It's frequently the lower-cost option for small firms.

### SMAS or CHAS — which should I get?

For a basic SSIP requirement they're interchangeable, as both are SSIP-approved.
SMAS often comes in cheaper for small contractors; CHAS is more widely named by
some clients. Check what your client accepts, then pick the cheaper qualifying
scheme.

### Does SMAS offer the Common Assessment Standard?

Yes — through its Worksafe Pro package, at the desktop assessment level. You only
need it if a client specifically asks for the Common Assessment Standard.
