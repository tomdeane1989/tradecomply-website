**CHAS (the Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme) is an SSIP-approved health & safety accreditation scheme that prices membership by employee headcount.** Standard membership starts at £429/year +VAT and provides the core SSIP badge most subcontractors need. Advanced (from £659/yr +VAT) adds PAS 91-style pre-qualification, and Elite (from £909/yr +VAT) adds the Common Assessment Standard across 13 risk areas. Unless a client specifically names those extra features, Standard is the package you want — buying up "to be safe" wastes money.

## Which package do you need?

<StepList>
<Step number={1}>**Just need SSIP / a health & safety badge?** Standard covers
it. It's the one most subcontractors buy.</Step>
<Step number={2}>**Client wants a PAS 91-style pre-qualification?** Advanced adds
the former PAS 91 standard and a PQQ system.</Step>
<Step number={3}>**Client names the Common Assessment Standard?** You need
**Elite**, which carries it across 13 risk areas. Nothing below Elite satisfies
a Common Assessment Standard requirement at CHAS. See the [Common Assessment Standard question set](/common-assessment-standard-question-set) to understand what that assessment covers.</Step>
</StepList>

<PlainEnglish>
Buy Standard unless a client specifically asks for more. The dearer packages add
pre-qualification depth (Advanced) and the Common Assessment Standard (Elite) —
useful only when someone actually requires them. Don't buy Elite "to be safe".
</PlainEnglish>

## Is CHAS the cheapest?

For the smallest businesses, **Constructionline's entry tier starts lower**
(around £219/year) and **SafeContractor Standard** is slightly below CHAS at
£419/year +VAT. But each scheme prices differently — CHAS and SafeContractor by
headcount, Constructionline by turnover — so the cheapest for *you* depends on
your size. Compare them in

## Frequently asked questions

### How much is CHAS membership per year?

From £429/year +VAT for the Standard package at the smallest headcount band,
rising with your employee count. Advanced starts at £659 and Elite at £909, both
+VAT. A free CHAS Foundation data-sharing account is also available.

### What's the difference between CHAS Standard, Advanced and Elite?

Standard is the core SSIP health & safety package. Advanced adds former PAS 91
compliance and a PQQ system. Elite adds the Common Assessment Standard across 13
risk areas. Most subcontractors only need Standard.

### Does CHAS include VAT?

No — CHAS prices are quoted **ex-VAT**, so add 20% VAT when budgeting.
