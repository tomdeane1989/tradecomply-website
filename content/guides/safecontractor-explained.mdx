**SafeContractor is an SSIP (Safety Schemes in Procurement)-approved health & safety assessment scheme run by Alcumus.** Like CHAS, it checks that your health & safety is in order and issues a certificate a main contractor can accept — and because it's SSIP-approved, that certificate is recognised by other SSIP schemes. It's one of the most commonly requested badges for UK subcontractors.

<Callout variant="info">
**TL;DR:** SafeContractor is Alcumus's SSIP-approved health & safety scheme, assessing your compliance against the same SSIP Core Criteria as CHAS and other member schemes. You complete a health & safety assessment (plus insurance checks), get a 12-month certificate, and choose from four plans starting at £419/year based on headcount. Because it's SSIP-approved, one SafeContractor certificate is recognised by other SSIP schemes under Deem to Satisfy — meaning you rarely need both SafeContractor and CHAS unless a client names a specific scheme.
</Callout>

## What SafeContractor actually checks

The assessment reviews your health & safety against the shared **SSIP Core Criteria**, plus category-specific questions for the type of work you do, and it verifies your insurance. Pass, and you get a certificate **valid for 12 months**.

<PlainEnglish>
It's the same core health & safety bar as CHAS and the other SSIP schemes — that sameness is the whole point of SSIP. So if a client just wants "SSIP", SafeContractor satisfies it exactly like CHAS would.
</PlainEnglish>

## Who asks for it, and is it worth it?

Plenty of main contractors and clients accept "any SSIP scheme", in which case SafeContractor is interchangeable with CHAS — pick whichever is cheaper for your size or faster to turn around. Some clients name SafeContractor specifically; if so, you need it by name. Either way, you rarely need it **and** another SSIP scheme: under [Deem to Satisfy](/ssip/ssip-mutual-recognition-explained) one valid SSIP certificate is recognised across members.

<WatchOut title="One SSIP scheme is usually enough">
If you already hold CHAS or another SSIP scheme, ask a client whether they'll accept it under Deem to Satisfy before paying for SafeContractor as well. Start from the [scheme comparison hub](/compare) if you're weighing your options.
</WatchOut>

## Frequently asked questions

### Is SafeContractor SSIP approved?

Yes. SafeContractor is an SSIP-approved scheme, so its health & safety certificate is recognised by other SSIP member schemes under Deem to Satisfy.

### Who runs SafeContractor?

SafeContractor is operated by Alcumus, a UK compliance and risk-management group. You'll sometimes see it branded "Alcumus SafeContractor".

### Is SafeContractor the same as CHAS?

For the core health & safety assessment, effectively yes — both are SSIP-approved and assess against the same SSIP Core Criteria. They differ on price, turnaround speed and which one a given client names. You don't normally need both.

### How long does SafeContractor last?

A SafeContractor certificate is valid for 12 months, then you renew with a fresh annual assessment.
