**SafeContractor (Alcumus's health & safety scheme) starts from £419/year** for
the Standard plan at the smallest headcount band, with assisted and
faster-turnaround plans from £489–£544. Prices scale by **employee headcount**,
and there's a **one-off joining fee** on top in your first year (the amount isn't
published — ask for it in your quote). All plans deliver SSIP (Safety Schemes in
Procurement)-approved certification valid for 12 months.

## The plans and prices

These are starting figures for the smallest headcount band, quoted **ex-VAT**.
The plans assess the same core standard — they mostly differ on **how fast** you
get seen and **how much help** you get with the application.

## What you actually get

Every plan delivers an **SSIP-approved** health & safety assessment, insurance
verification, and category-specific risk questions, with a certificate valid for
12 months. Because it's SSIP-approved, that certificate is recognised by other
SSIP schemes under [Deem to Satisfy](/ssip/ssip-mutual-recognition-explained) —
so you rarely need a second scheme as well.

<PlainEnglish>
You're not paying more for a "better" assessment on the dearer plans — you're
paying for **speed and hand-holding**. If you're comfortable filling in the
forms and aren't against a tender deadline, the Standard plan gets you the same
SSIP badge for the least money.
</PlainEnglish>

## Is it the cheapest option?

Not always. For the smallest businesses, **Constructionline's entry tier starts
lower** (around £219/year), and **CHAS Standard** is broadly comparable at
£429/year +VAT. Which is cheapest for *you* depends on your size, because each
scheme prices differently — SafeContractor and CHAS by headcount,
Constructionline by turnover. Compare them in
[CHAS vs SafeContractor vs Constructionline](/compare/chas-vs-safecontractor-vs-constructionline)
and check [CHAS cost](/compare/chas-cost). If you're still deciding which badge
you even need, start at the [comparison hub](/compare).

## Frequently asked questions

### How much is SafeContractor per year?

From £419/year +VAT for the Standard plan at the smallest headcount band, rising
with your employee count, plus a one-off joining fee in year one. Assisted,
Express and Premier plans start at £489–£544 for faster turnaround or application
support.

### Is there a joining fee for SafeContractor?

Yes — SafeContractor adds a one-off joining fee to the first year's membership.
The amount isn't published on the pricing page, so request it as part of your
quote — SafeContractor doesn't publish the joining-fee amount.

### Does SafeContractor include VAT in its prices?

No — SafeContractor's published prices are quoted **ex-VAT**, so add 20% VAT to
budget the real cost.
